1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Daltaners is a Filipino online-to-offline (O2O) e-commerce and groceries platform 
designed to connect local stores (sari-sari stores, Kirana-equivalent partners, 
supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, and specialty retailers) with consumers 
across the Philippines. Inspired by the architecture and operational excellence 
of Amazon Fresh and Carrefour Marketplace, Daltaners leverages a hyperlocal, 
data-driven approach to optimize supply chain logistics, minimize delivery times, 
and provide a seamless multi-vendor marketplace experience.

The platform supports multiple service verticals including grocery delivery, 
food delivery, pharmacy delivery, parcel delivery, and car rental services. 
Daltaners differentiates itself through AI-powered inventory management, WhatsApp 
integration for orders, zone-wise delivery management, and a robust POS system 
for physical store operations.

Target Market: Philippines (initial), with expansion roadmap to Southeast Asia.
Expected Daily Transactions: 1,000,000+ at scale.
Delivery Promise: Express delivery within 2-4 hours; standard within 24 hours.

2. PRODUCT VISION & MISSION
================================================================================

VISION:
To become the Philippines' most trusted and efficient hyperlocal commerce 
platform, empowering every local store to compete in the digital economy while 
providing Filipino consumers with fast, affordable, and convenient access to 
everything they need.

MISSION:
- Bridge the gap between local brick-and-mortar stores and digital consumers
- Democratize e-commerce by enabling small merchants (sari-sari stores, local 
  markets) to reach customers beyond their physical footprint
- Deliver unmatched convenience through AI-optimized logistics and hyperlocal 
  fulfillment
- Build a sustainable ecosystem that benefits merchants, delivery partners, and 
  consumers equally

CORE VALUES:
- Speed: Minimize time from order to delivery
- Accessibility: Every Filipino, regardless of location, can access the platform
- Trust: Transparent pricing, no-questions-asked returns, secure payments
- Empowerment: Enable local businesses to thrive digitally
- Innovation: Continuously improve through AI, data, and technology

5. BUSINESS MODEL & REVENUE STREAMS
================================================================================

5.1 REVENUE STREAMS
--------------------------------------
1. Commission per Transaction: 10-25% commission on each order (varies by 
   category: groceries 10-15%, food 20-25%, electronics 8-12%)
2. Delivery Fees: Charged to customer based on distance/zone
3. Vendor Subscription Plans:
   - Free Tier: Basic listing, 20% commission
   - Silver: Enhanced visibility, 15% commission, ₱999/month
   - Gold: Priority placement, 12% commission, analytics, ₱2,499/month
   - Platinum: Full features, 10% commission, dedicated support, ₱4,999/month
4. Advertising Revenue:
   - Sponsored listings
   - Banner ads on customer app
   - Featured store placement
5. POS License Fee: Monthly fee for in-store POS hardware/software
6. Car Rental Commission: 15-20% per rental transaction
7. Premium Features:
   - Priority delivery for customers (subscription)
   - Advanced analytics for merchants
   - API access for enterprise integrations
8. Financial Services (Future):
   - Merchant lending
   - Customer BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later)
   - Digital wallet float

5.2 PRICING STRATEGY
--------------------------------------
DELIVERY PRICING:
- Free delivery: Orders above ₱500 minimum order value
- Standard delivery (below minimum): ₱49-99 based on distance/zone
- Express delivery (2-4 hours): ₱99-149
- Scheduled delivery: ₱39-79

ORDER MINIMUMS:
- Grocery: ₱200 minimum order
- Food: ₱150 minimum order
- Pharmacy: No minimum
- Parcel: Per-item pricing
